How do I get my NPSSO?

  1. Log into the Playstation website
  2. Visit this page: https://ca.account.sony.com/api/v1/ssocookie
  3. Copy the npsso token and paste it into this page and hit enter

(NPSSO tokens automatically get deleted after 2 months by Sony or when you make a new login attempt)
(This website does not store your token externally, all source code is available on Github)
